About Grace Wambui.

Hello! My name is Grace Wambui and I am a budding full-stack developer, singer, songwriter and music producer.

I've been performing since 2016 after graduating from an artist development program called 'Sauti Academy'. While in attendance at the program, I studied songwriting, vocal technique and music business.

I've also been programing beats and recording music. I started making beats on FLStudio,a popular digital audio workstation when I was 16years old.
